EXPLANATION FOR THE SOLAR TRACKING SYSTEM :

  • A solar tracking system is a mechanism that allows solar panels to follow the path of the sun as it moves across the sky.
  • This tracking helps maximize the absorption of sunlight by keeping the solar panels oriented directly towards the sun ,optimizing energy capture throughout the day .
  • The typically includes sensors or motors that adjust the position of the solar panels to ensure they are always facing the sun , increasing over all energy efficiency .

MERITS :

  • Trackers generate more electricity than their stationary counterparts due to increased direct exposure to solar rays. This increase can be as much as 10 to 25% depending on the geographic location of the tracking system.
  • Solar tracking systems include increased energy production due to optimized sunlight exposure, improved efficiency, and potential for better integration with energy storage.

DEMERITS :

  • Higher initial costs, increased maintenance requirements, and the complexity of tracking mechanisms.
  • Solar trackers are generally designed for climates with little to no snow making them a more viable solution in warmer climates.
  • Fixed racking accommodates harsher environmental conditions more easily than tracking systems.
